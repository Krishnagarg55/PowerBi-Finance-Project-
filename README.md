📊 AI-Driven Cryptocurrency Market Analysis Dashboard | Power BI
🚀 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides an AI-enhanced cryptocurrency market analysis and prediction system, combining historical financial data, market performance metrics, and AI-generated forecasting insights to support strategic investment decisions.

The project integrates research-backed datasets from Perplexity AI and future prediction analysis using Perplexity AI & Claude AI, transforming raw market information into meaningful business insights and predictive intelligence.

🎯 Business Problem

Cryptocurrency markets are highly volatile, making it difficult for investors and analysts to:

Identify profitable investment opportunities
Predict future market movements
Understand risk-adjusted returns
Compare asset performance effectively
Monitor volatility and price fluctuations
Make data-driven decisions using historical trends

This dashboard addresses these challenges through interactive visualization, financial KPIs, and AI-assisted predictive analytics.

📌 Objectives

The dashboard aims to:

✔ Analyze cryptocurrency market performance
✔ Track ROI and price movement trends
✔ Evaluate market volatility and risk
✔ Compare investment performance across assets
✔ Generate AI-based future predictions
✔ Deliver actionable business insights for investors
